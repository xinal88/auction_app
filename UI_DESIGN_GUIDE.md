## Visual Design Overview

This document describes the visual design and user experience of the Modern Auction Application.

## ğŸ¯ Design Philosophy

### Minimalist & Professional
- **Clean layouts** with generous white space
- **Card-based design** for content organization
- **Subtle shadows** for depth without distraction
- **Rounded corners** for modern, friendly feel

### Color Psychology
- **Indigo (#4F46E5)**: Trust, professionalism, primary actions
- **Green (#10B981)**: Success, money, positive outcomes
- **Amber (#F59E0B)**: Warning, urgency, attention
- **Red (#EF4444)**: Danger, errors, critical states
